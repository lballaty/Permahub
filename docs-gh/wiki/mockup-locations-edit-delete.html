How It Works

Your Locations

When you're logged in and viewing locations you created:

  • Green "You created this" badge appears in location cards
  • Green border around your location cards
  • Light blue action area with Edit and Delete buttons
  • Map popups include Edit/Delete buttons for your markers

Other Users' Locations

When viewing locations created by others:

  • No ownership badge or special borders
  • Only "View on Map" and "Full Details" buttons
  • No edit or delete permissions

Location Management Features

Managing your locations:

  • Edit - Update address, coordinates, description, contact info, hours
  • Delete - Remove locations that are no longer active
  • Status - Mark as draft, published, or archived
  • Map Integration - Edit/delete directly from map popups
