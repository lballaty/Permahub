## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy ("Policy") explains how Permaculture Network ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and platform (collectively, the "Platform"). This Policy applies alongside our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our Platform. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and improve your experience.

---

## 2. What Are Cookies?

### 2.1 Definition
A cookie is a small file consisting of text and numbers that is downloaded onto your device when you access a website or application. Cookies are stored on your device and sent back to the server on subsequent visits.

### 2.2 How Cookies Work
When you visit our Platform:
1. Your browser requests pages from our server
2. Our server sends the page along with cookies
3. Your browser stores these cookies locally
4. On your next visit, your browser sends cookies back to the server
5. The server recognizes you based on the cookie data

### 2.3 Cookie Lifespan
- **Session Cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period (days, months, or years)
- **Expiration:** Cookies automatically delete after their set expiration date

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential/Strictly Necessary Cookies

**Purpose:** Required for the Platform to function

**Examples:**
- **Session Authentication:** Keeps you logged in during your visit
- **Security Cookies:** Prevents unauthorized access and fraud
- **User Preferences:** Remembers settings you choose
- **Form Data:** Preserves information in forms while you complete them
- **Load Balancing:** Distributes server traffic for optimal performance
- **CSRF Protection:** Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks

**Duration:** Typically session-based (cleared on browser close)

**Legal Basis:** Necessary for contract performance (providing our services)

**Can Be Disabled?** No - disabling these will prevent the Platform from functioning

**Examples:**
- `auth_token` - Authenticates your login session
- `session_id` - Identifies your current session
- `csrf_token` - Protects against CSRF attacks
- `platform_lang` - Remembers your language preference

---

### 3.2 Preference/Functionality Cookies

**Purpose:** Remember your choices and preferences to enhance your experience

**Examples:**
- **Language Selection:** Your preferred language or locale
- **Accessibility Settings:** Font sizes, color schemes, screen reader settings
- **Display Preferences:** Dark mode vs. light mode
- **UI Customization:** Which panels or widgets you have enabled
- **Search Filters:** Previously used search criteria
- **Layout Preferences:** Compact vs. expanded views

**Duration:** 1 year to 2 years

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest in providing better user experience

**Can Be Disabled?** Yes - but some features may not work optimally

**Examples:**
- `ui_theme` - Dark/light mode preference
- `language_preference` - Your selected language
- `accessibility_settings` - Text size, contrast, other accessibility options
- `map_zoom_level` - Default zoom for map views

---

### 3.3 Analytics & Performance Cookies

**Purpose:** Understand how users interact with the Platform to improve it

**Examples:**
- **Page Performance:** Load times, page responsiveness
- **User Behavior:** Which features are most used, navigation patterns
- **Error Tracking:** Technical errors and bugs
- **Traffic Analysis:** How users find and navigate the Platform
- **Device Information:** Device types, browsers, screen sizes

**Providers:**
- Google Analytics (if enabled)
- Sentry (error tracking)
- Custom analytics scripts

**Duration:** 13 months (Google Analytics default)

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest in understanding and improving our services

**Can Be Disabled?** Yes - through cookie settings

**Data Shared:** Anonymized, aggregated data sent to analytics providers

**Examples:**
- `_ga` - Google Analytics identifier
- `_gid` - Google Analytics session identifier
- `_gat` - Google Analytics throttling
- `event_tracking` - Custom event data

**GDPR Note:** Analytics cookies require consent before placement

---

**Purpose:** Track conversions and show relevant content (only with consent)

**Examples:**
- **Conversion Tracking:** Track if users complete desired actions (signup, project creation)
- **Retargeting:** Show relevant ads if you leave our Platform
- **Campaign Performance:** Measure effectiveness of marketing campaigns
- **User Segmentation:** Group users for targeted messaging
- **Advertising Networks:** Integration with ad platforms

**Providers:**
- Google Ads
- Facebook Pixel
- Other advertising networks (if enabled)

**Duration:** 6 months to 2 years

**Legal Basis:** Consent (required for EU users)

**Can Be Disabled?** Yes - through cookie settings and privacy controls

**Data Shared:** Limited data sent to advertising platforms

**GDPR Note:** These cookies require explicit opt-in consent

**Examples:**
- `_fbp` - Facebook Pixel identifier
- `_fbc` - Facebook conversion tracking
- `gclid` - Google Ads conversion tracking
- `conversion_data` - Custom conversion events

## 4. How We Use Cookie Data

### 4.1 Purposes
We use cookies to:
- **Authenticate users** and maintain login sessions
- **Remember preferences** (language, theme, settings)
- **Prevent fraud** and maintain security
- **Improve performance** and identify technical issues
- **Understand usage patterns** to enhance the Platform
- **Measure effectiveness** of features and updates
- **Personalize content** based on interests
- **Track conversions** from marketing campaigns (with consent)

### 4.2 Non-Identification
Cookies do not identify you personally. They use:
- Anonymized identifiers
- Session tokens
- Preference codes
- Behavioral signals (not linked to your name or email)

Even with cookies, we cannot determine your real identity unless you log in.

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Essential Cookies:** Deleted after session or 1-2 years
- **Preference Cookies:** Retained for 1-2 years
- **Analytics Cookies:** Retained for 13 months (Google Analytics standard)
- **Marketing Cookies:** Retained for 6 months to 2 years

After expiration, cookies are automatically deleted from your device.

## 5. Cookie Consent & Management

### 5.1 Consent Banner
When you first visit our Platform, you will see a cookie consent banner with options to:
- **Accept All Cookies:** Accepts essential, preference, analytics, and marketing cookies
- **Reject All:** Accepts only essential cookies (required for functionality)
- **Manage Preferences:** Choose which non-essential cookies to allow
- **Cookie Settings:** Link to detailed preferences

### 5.2 Consent Choices

**Essential Only:**
- Session authentication
- Security protection
- Form functionality
- Platform basic operation

**Essential + Preferences:**
- All above PLUS
- Language and theme settings
- Accessibility preferences
- Layout customization

**Essential + Analytics:**
- All above PLUS
- Google Analytics
- Error tracking
- Performance monitoring

**All Cookies:**
- Essential + Preferences + Analytics + Marketing

### 5.3 Changing Your Consent

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:
1. Going to **Settings > Privacy > Cookie Settings**
2. Using your browser's built-in cookie management tools
3. Clicking "Cookie Settings" in the website footer
4. Accessing the cookie consent banner (bottom of page)

Changes take effect immediately.

## 6. How to Control Cookies

### 6.1 Browser Controls

**Chrome:**
1. Click the menu (three dots) → Settings
2. Click Privacy and security → Third-party cookies
3. Choose your preference

**Firefox:**
1. Click the menu (three lines) → Preferences
2. Go to Privacy & Security
3. Find "Cookies and Site Data"
4. Choose your preference

**Safari:**
1. Click Safari → Preferences
2. Click Privacy
3. Choose "Cookies and website data" options

**Edge:**
1. Click the menu (three dots) → Settings
2. Go to Privacy and search
3. Find "Cookies and other site data"
4. Choose your preference

**Important:** Disabling cookies may prevent the Platform from functioning properly.

### 6.2 Opt-Out Tools

**Google Analytics Opt-Out:**
- Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Extension
- Available for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
- Prevents Google Analytics from tracking your visits

**Online Preference Tools:**
- Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): https://optout.aboutads.info/
- Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): https://optout.networkadvertising.org/
- EDAA (Europe): https://www.youronlinechoices.com/

### 6.3 Do Not Track (DNT)

Many browsers include a "Do Not Track" setting. Currently, our Platform does not respond to DNT signals, but you can:
- Enable DNT in your browser settings
- Use platform cookie settings to control tracking
- Opt-out of specific cookie types

## 7. GDPR Compliance

### 7.1 Legal Basis for Cookies

Under GDPR, cookies are only lawful if we have a legal basis:

**Necessary (No Consent Required):**
- Essential/strictly necessary cookies
- Required for service delivery
- Cannot be disabled

**Legitimate Interest (Transparency Required):**
- Preference cookies
- Analytics cookies
- Performance monitoring
- Privacy Policy must disclose use

**Consent Required:**
- Marketing/advertising cookies
- Retargeting cookies
- Campaign tracking
- Explicit opt-in required before placement

**Contract:**
- Cookies needed for service you requested
- Part of our agreement with you

### 7.2 User Rights (GDPR Articles 21-22)

You have the right to:
- **Withdraw Consent:** Refuse or revoke marketing cookies at any time
- **Object:** Request we stop using certain cookies
- **Access:** Know what cookies we use and why
- **Portability:** Receive cookie-related data in portable format
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of cookie data (for some types)

### 7.3 Cookies for Children

If users under 16 access our Platform:
- We do NOT use marketing or tracking cookies
- Only essential and preference cookies are set
- Parental consent may be required
- No profiling or automated decision-making

## 9. Cookie Security

### 9.1 Protection Measures

- **HTTPS Only:** Cookies transmitted only over encrypted connections
- **Secure Flag:** Cookies sent only over HTTPS, never HTTP
- **HttpOnly Flag:** Cookies inaccessible to JavaScript (prevents XSS attacks)
- **SameSite Attribute:** Cookies not sent in cross-site requests (prevents CSRF)
- **Encryption:** Sensitive data within cookies is encrypted

### 9.2 What Cookies Don't Contain

Our cookies never include:
- Passwords
- Credit card information
- Personal identification numbers
- Health information
- Financial details
- Biometric data
- Any other sensitive information
