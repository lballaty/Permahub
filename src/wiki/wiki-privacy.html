1. Introduction

Welcome to Permahub Community Wiki ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Registration:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Location (city, region, country)
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Website/social media links (optional)

Profile Information:

  • Bio and biography
  • Profile avatar/photo
  • Skills and expertise
  • Interests and areas of focus
  • Profile visibility settings

Content & Communication:

  • Guide content, descriptions, and images
  • Event listings and details
  • Messages you send through our platform
  • Support requests and inquiries
  • Feedback and survey responses
  • Comments on guides or events

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Technical Information:

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Links clicked on our site
  • Search queries within our platform

Location Data:

  • Geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) from your profile
  • Location-based search queries
  • Device location (if you grant permission)

Cookies & Similar Technologies:

  • Session cookies for authentication
  • Preference cookies for user experience
  • Analytics cookies for site performance

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication providers (email verification services)
  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Payment processors (if applicable)
  • Social media platforms (if you link your accounts)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Service Delivery

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Delivering the platform's features and functionality
  • Processing transactions and payments
  • Verifying your identity and preventing fraud
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests

3.2 User Experience Enhancement

  • Personalizing content and recommendations
  • Analyzing platform usage and performance
  • Improving our services and features
  • Conducting research and analytics

3.3 Communication

  • Sending service-related announcements
  • Notifying you of updates to policies or services
  • Responding to your requests and feedback
  • Sending administrative information

3.4 Networking & Discovery

  • Connecting you with other users based on interests and location
  • Displaying your profile to other users (based on your privacy settings)
  • Facilitating community collaboration
  • Recommending guides or events relevant to your interests

3.5 Legal & Safety

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Protecting against fraud and abuse
  • Maintaining platform security
  • Responding to legal requests from authorities

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Public Information

Information you choose to make public on your profile will be visible to all registered platform users and visitors to the platform (depending on your settings). This includes: name, bio, skills, interests, guides, events, and any other information marked as "public."

4.2 Service Providers

We share necessary information with third-party service providers who assist us:

  • Cloud hosting providers (Supabase)
  • Email and communication services
  • Analytics services
  • Customer support platforms

These providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only to provide our services.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations or court orders, enforce our Terms of Service, protect the safety, rights, and property of the Company, users, or the public, or prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing.

4.4 Aggregated & De-Identified Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you for research and analytics, platform statistics and reports, improving services, and marketing purposes.

5. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

5.1 Access & Portability

You have the right to access all personal information we hold about you and request a copy of your data in a portable format.

To request: Contact us at privacy@permahub.org

5.2 Correction & Updates

You can update your profile information at any time through your account settings.

5.3 Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Upon deletion, your personal profile is removed, your email and login credentials are deleted, and your private content is removed.

To request: Go to Account Settings > Delete Account or contact us.

5.4 Privacy Settings

You control the visibility of your information:

  • Make your profile public or private
  • Choose which information is visible to other users
  • Opt-out of recommendations and networking features
  • Control location sharing preferences

5.5 Communications Preferences

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails, opt-out of push notifications, manage communication frequency preferences, and disable activity notifications.

5.6 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings: accept or reject non-essential cookies, delete cookies at any time, or disable tracking cookies.

Note: Some cookies are necessary for core functionality. Disabling them may affect your ability to use the platform.

6. Data Retention

6.1 Active Account Data

While your account is active, we retain your information to provide our services.

6.2 Deleted Account Data

  • Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days
  • Personal data is deleted from our systems
  • Backup copies are retained for 90 days for disaster recovery
  • After 90 days, all traces are permanently removed
  • Audit logs may be retained for compliance purposes (max 1 year)

6.3 Legal Holds

If required by law, we may retain information longer than our standard retention periods.

6.4 Aggregated Data

Anonymized data is retained indefinitely for analytics and research.

7. Data Security

7.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security practices:

  • Encryption: All data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication: Secure login with password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Access Control: Role-based access restrictions on databases
  • Row-Level Security: Database policies prevent unauthorized data access
  • Firewalls: Network-level protection against attacks
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Monitoring: Real-time security monitoring and alerting

7.2 Limitations

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

7.3 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you without unreasonable delay, provide details about what occurred, describe steps we're taking to secure your data, and offer resources to help protect yourself.

8. International Data Transfers

8.1 Data Localization

  • Primary data storage: European Union (Supabase infrastructure)
  • Backup storage: Geographically distributed for redundancy
  • Data may be transferred internationally as needed for service delivery

8.2 Compliance

For users in the European Union:

  • We comply with GDPR requirements
  • Standard Contractual Clauses are in place for international transfers
  • We implement appropriate safeguards for your rights

For users in other jurisdictions:

  • We comply with applicable local privacy laws
  • We implement equivalent privacy protections globally

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately and terminate the child's account.

10. Third-Party Links & Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services before providing your information.

11. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

11.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Session authentication, security and fraud prevention, basic functionality

Preference Cookies: Language and display settings, user interface preferences, accessibility settings

Analytics Cookies: Usage statistics, performance monitoring, error tracking

11.2 Cookie Consent

  • We obtain explicit consent for non-essential cookies
  • You can withdraw consent and delete cookies at any time
  • Cookie preferences can be changed in your account settings

12. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Oppose certain processing activities
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your data protection authority
  • Right Related to Automated Decision-Making: Object to automated decisions

To exercise these rights: Contact privacy@permahub.org

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: What personal information is collected and how it's used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of "sale" of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: No discrimination for exercising privacy rights
  • Right to Limit: Limit use of sensitive personal information

To exercise these rights: Contact privacy@permahub.org
