Introduction

Welcome to the Permaculture Network ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Email address, full name, password, location, phone number (optional), website/social media links
  • Profile Information: Bio, avatar/photo, skills, interests, looking for, visibility settings
  • Project & Resource Information: Project details, images, geographic coordinates, contact information
  • Communication: Messages, support requests, feedback, comments
  • Payment Information: Handled securely by third-party providers (we don't store this)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, search queries
  • Location Data: Geographic coordinates from your profile, location-based searches
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Creating accounts, delivering features, processing transactions
  • Communication: Service announcements, policy updates, support responses
  • Enhancement: Personalizing content, analyzing usage, improving services
  • Networking: Connecting you with other users, recommending projects and resources
  • Marketing: Newsletters and announcements (with your consent)
  • Legal & Safety: Compliance, fraud prevention, security, legal requests

How We Share Your Information

Public Information

Information you mark as public will be visible to all users and potentially search engines. This includes your name, bio, skills, interests, and projects.

Service Providers

We share necessary information with third-party providers (Supabase, email services, payment processors) who are contractually bound to protect your information.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect safety, rights, and property.

Aggregated & De-Identified Data

We may share anonymized data for research, analytics, and improvements.

Your Privacy Rights & Choices

Access & Portability

You can request a copy of all data we hold about you. Email: privacy@permaculturenetwork.org

Correction & Updates

Update your profile information anytime through your account settings.

Deletion

Request deletion of your account and associated data. This is processed within 30 days. Go to Account Settings > Delete Account.

Privacy Settings

Control the visibility of your information:

  • Make your profile public or private
  • Choose which information is visible
  • Opt-out of recommendations
  • Control location sharing

Communications Preferences

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails (one-click link in every email)
  • Opt-out of push notifications
  • Manage communication frequency
  • Disable activity notifications

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit
  • Authentication: Secure login with bcrypt hashing
  • Access Control: Role-based restrictions and row-level security
  • Firewalls: Network-level protection
  • Monitoring: Real-time security monitoring
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing

Important: While we implement robust security, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining confidentiality of your login credentials.

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive data in portable format
  • Right to Object: Oppose certain processing
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File with your data protection authority

Contact Us

Privacy Inquiries:

  • Email: privacy@permaculturenetwork.org
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@permaculturenetwork.org

Response Time: We respond to privacy requests within 30 days.
